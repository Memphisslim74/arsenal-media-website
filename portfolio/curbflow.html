Project overview

CurbFlow started as a simple landscape curbing sales and scheduling demo. The latest update expands it into a full command suite for the way curbing contractors actually work: lead intake, footage-based estimating, profile and color selections, quote design, approvals, schedule handoff, production readiness, and crew work orders.

The problem this solves

Decorative curbing businesses need details that generic CRMs do not understand: footage, profile, color, pattern, prep notes, photos, deposits, crews, and install readiness.
Sales follow-up, estimates, scheduling, and job handoff often happen in separate tools or text threads, which makes it easy to miss details.
The demo needed to stay public and easy to test while still feeling like a serious product.

What was built

Sales Command board with customer movement across lead, estimate, approval, and production stages.
Customer Tracker with source, stage, quote value, next follow-up, and job status visibility.
Landscape curbing estimate builder with footage, profiles, add-ons, and pricing-controlled options.
Quote Designer with draggable sections, saved templates, branded layouts, and Canva upload support.
Job Readiness board and printable Work Orders for office-to-crew handoff.
Route planner, team/crew views, reviews/referrals, documents, approvals, change orders, and admin controls.

Why it matters

Turns a niche contractor workflow into a focused demo instead of a generic CRM.
Makes the sales-to-production handoff clearer for owners, office staff, and crews.
Gives prospective buyers a better sense of how a custom app can be shaped around their exact business process.
