Questions

Questions before starting.

Good projects start with plain answers. Here are the things most business owners ask before getting into the details.

What kind of custom apps can Arsenal Media build?

Arsenal Media builds web-based business apps such as CRMs, dashboards, estimators, work order tools, customer trackers, admin portals, and reporting systems.

Can we start with a demo before a production build?

Yes. Starting with a demo is often the best move because it lets you test the workflow, screens, and language before building the full system.

Do custom apps work on phones and tablets?

Yes. Every new build should be responsive so office staff, owners, and field teams can use it from the devices they already have.

Can the app connect to email, forms, or other tools?

Usually, yes. The right approach depends on the tool and whether it has an API, webhook, export, or database connection available.

How much does a custom app cost?

It depends on the workflow, number of users, data structure, integrations, and production requirements. A focused demo costs much less than a full production platform.
