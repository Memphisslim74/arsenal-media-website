Questions

Questions before starting.

Good projects start with plain answers. Here are the things most business owners ask before getting into the details.

What is the first SEO fix most service websites need?

Most need clearer service pages, better titles and descriptions, proper headings, local service area language, and stronger internal links.

Does SEO include technical files like robots.txt and sitemap.xml?

Yes. Those files help crawlers find the right pages and understand what should be indexed.

Can SEO help with AI search results?

It can help by making your site easier to crawl and understand. Clear service pages, schema, FAQs, and plain-language answers give AI search tools better context.

Should every service have its own page?

For most businesses, yes. Separate service pages are easier to rank, link to, share, and explain than one page that tries to cover everything.

Can SEO be added to an existing website?

Yes. Metadata, schema, internal links, content structure, and technical files can be improved without rebuilding the whole site in many cases.
